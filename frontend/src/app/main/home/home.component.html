Grad Management

Grad management system for handling the grads by admin

Functionalities:
  • Records the details of the new employees.
  • Admin can see and filter the details of all the employees based on employee Id.
  • Admin can click on an employee id to visit the profile page of the employee

Management helps in organizing the personal information of the new employees in a structured manner. As it creates a profile page of each of the new employees based on the details entered by him/her. And the admin can see the details of all new employees joining in a particular session.


Course Management

Managing the courses

  • Add/Delete course.
  • Add/Delete trainer and their corresponding courses.
  • Add/Modify batch.
  • Add/Delete session in a batch.
  • View the timesheet.

Course Management helps the grads in viewing their timesheet for the entire training period. It also provides admin an easy way to manage sessions for different batches.


Employee Demand

Checking the employee and his demand as a trainer

Functions:
  • Add employee and their roles.
  • Add demand for particular ED at certain location.
  • View the demand details for all the ED's.

It will add the new employee and their roles.It can select the ED,HM and demand count at particular location.The demand details can be viewed in structured manner.


Score

Score calculations of AU batch

Functionalities:
  • Displays the assessment scores and ranking of grads
  • Filter employee details based on employee name
  • Enables to enter the details of tests given by grads

Assessment Management module records and displays the assessment details of employees. Filters can be applied to fetch the details of tests given by particular employee. Details of new tests given by grads can also be added.
